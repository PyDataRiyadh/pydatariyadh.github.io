About the Chapter

Riyadh’s chapter of PyData, The monthly meetups are put together by a group of volunteers passionate about Python and open source data science tools. Since 2020, we have provided data science enthusiast in Riyadh a forum to learn from each other on the use of open source languages in the ever-evolving fields of data management, processing, analytics and visualisation. PyData Riyadh meetups take place on the first Tuesday of each month, currently under the generous sponsorship from ____.

About the speakers

All speakers at PyData Riyadh meetups do so voluntarily! A big thank you to all speakers who volunteer their time to prepare amazing content to educate and enlighten fellow data science enthusiast. We welcome speakers from all levels and backgrounds - we welcome you to submit a talk!

PyData Riyadh Organisers

Behind the scene, unpaid volunteers organise the logistics and content of every meetup. The organisers have day jobs to pay their bills and dedicate their free time for the benefit of the community. So when you see the organisers at the next event, don’t forget to thank them and have a good chat with them over coffee.
